Why ng-grid?

ng-grid offers many compelling features that are used commonly in grids today such as: grouping, resizable and reorderable columns, sorting, cell and row templates, built-in angular filters, and much more! Learn more...

Extensible

With ng-grid, you can create plugins that can enhance the capabilities of the grid! Want to extend functionality? Make a plugin!
